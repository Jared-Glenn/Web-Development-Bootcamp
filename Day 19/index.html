ToDo List

You will need to open the console to see your results. (Shift + CTRL + k)

  • New: Type "new" to add an item to your ToDo List.
  • List: Type "list" to show a current list of your ToDo items and their indices.
  • Delete: Type "delete" to remove an item from your ToDo List.
  • Quit: Type "quit" to end the program. (All data on your list will be lost.)
